Liangliang Zhang, PhD

CINEMA Group CWRU Profile E-mail Twitter LinkedIn YouTube ORCID Google Scholar

Liangliang Zhang is a scholar and educator working at the intersection of biostatistics and microbiome science to advance data-driven discovery in human health! He is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Population and Quantitative Health Sciences, School of Medicine, Case Western Reserve University.

Education

  • PhD in Statistics (2017): Department of Statistics and Probability, Michigan State University. Advisors: Taps Maiti and Chae Young Lim.

  • MS in Statistics (2015): Department of Statistics and Probability, Michigan State University.

  • MS in Financial Mathematics (2012): Center for Financial Engineering, Soochow University, China. Advisors: Chenglong Xu and Guojing Wang.

  • BS in Statistics (2009): Department of Statistics, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecoms, China.
